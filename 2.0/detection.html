Quick Mood Self-Check

Answer the following 8 questions honestly. This is not a medical diagnosis but a quick check to understand how you might be feeling.

1. In the past two weeks, how often have you felt little interest or pleasure in doing things?

2. In the past two weeks, how often have you felt down, depressed, or hopeless?

3. In the past two weeks, how often have you felt nervous, anxious or on edge?

4. In the past two weeks, how often have you had trouble sleeping or sleeping too much?

5. In the past two weeks, how often have you felt tired or had little energy?

6. In the past two weeks, how often have you had trouble concentrating on things, such as reading or watching something?

7. In the past two weeks, how often have you felt that you were moving or speaking so slowly or being so restless that other people noticed?

8. In the past two weeks, how often have you had thoughts that you would be better off dead or of hurting yourself?
